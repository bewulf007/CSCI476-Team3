Advanced Robotics Camp


    Date: June 27th - July 1st
    Cost: $200

    Description:

This camp is for learners who have previous experience building robots. Perhaps, students who are returning to our Robotics Camps or students who have experienced competing in Robotics competitions. In this camp students will build their very own Robots and not be limited to the basic bot. Robots in all of their sizes, shapes, and levels of complexity are vital to our everyday livesâ€”everything from the car you drive to the energy it runs on is dependent in some way on the power of robotics and mechanical engineering. At our Advanced Robotics camp kids will work in teams, experiment, and create in small classes. We will continue to learn to program using Alice, a 3D programming environment, but begin to focus on more complex code, such as writing functions and making function calls. The programming language behind Alice is JavaScript.
