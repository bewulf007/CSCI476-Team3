Application Process


    1. Submit an online application. (A confirmation email will be sent to you to confirm receipt of your application. This is NOT your acceptance email.)
    2. An acceptance email will be sent to you. Once received, we ask that you make your payment for the camp fee to secure your child's space in our camp. You will have 2 weeks from the date of the acceptance email to make your payment. If your child was not accepted, you will receive notification from us.
    3. Limited financial aid will be available through the scholarship program.

Please note that not everyone that applies will be accepted into our summer camp programs. Camp selections are not done on a first come first served basis.

Cancellation Policy

No refunds will be issued once class begins. If a cancellation is done more than 2 weeks in advance, we will provide a full refund minus the $20 for handling application and camp preperation.
